Services Dance with us.

Private Party

Are you in need of an exciting and hassle-free music solution for your upcoming event? For a flat fee, our team will handle all aspects of the music portion of your event, ensuring that your guests have a memorable and enjoyable experience. And with our customizable playlist options and wireless headphones, we can cater to a wide range of music tastes and create the perfect atmosphere for your occasion.

Whether you're hosting a corporate event, wedding reception, or private party, DoCo Disco has the expertise and equipment to make your event a success. So why wait? Contact us today to learn more about how we can cater to your specific needs and bring the party to life!

Public Event

Is your bar or venue interested in having a unique DJ experience? We understand that planning an event can be stressful, which is why we strive to make the process as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Our team is committed to helping you every step of the way, from creating custom marketing materials to handling ticket sales at the door.

Doco Disco gets their profits from the ticket sales, and the venue gets their profits from the food and beverage sales.
